Welcome!

We’re glad you found us. Here you can find basic information about our annual event and learn how to get involved!!

About

THE Geek Fest (TGF) is an exciting and eclectic annual celebration of the “geek” in all of us - an event designed to educate, energize, and inspire the minds of students and community members by highlighting the creative innovations in technology, media, and industry. It recognizes the imaginative efforts made by individuals and companies in their respective fields and provides a venue to explore the technologies that have become so integral to our everyday lives.

Participate

THE Geek Fest is a community driven event, so we depend on the help and creativity of a lot of folks. We are looking for speakers, exhibitors, sponsors and volunteers. Please complete our short form to get started.
